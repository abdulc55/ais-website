# Spiffy Tec Sales Playbook

Updated: April 12, 2026

## Goal

Use Spiffy Tec to win recurring local-business clients on monthly website plans, then grow revenue through SEO, AI, and platform add-ons.

## Core Offer

Lead with outcome, not deliverable:

> We build and manage your website for one monthly price so you can get more calls, bookings, and trust from local customers.

Default recommendation:

- `Starter`: $99/mo
- `Business`: $149/mo
- `Growth`: $249/mo
- `AI Business Suite`: +$99/mo
- `Mobile App`: +$99/mo
- `SEO Package`: +$149/mo

Primary close target:

- Sell `Business` by default.
- Use `Starter` only for price-sensitive leads.
- Use `Growth` when the business needs payments, customer accounts, or deeper automation.

## Best First Niches

Start with one niche for 30 days:

- car detailing
- pressure washing
- lawn care
- house cleaning
- plumbers
- electricians

Why these work:

- They already buy leads.
- A single new job often covers monthly site cost.
- Many have weak sites, weak Google Business Profiles, or no clear booking path.

## 30-Day Sprint

### Week 1

- Pick one niche and one city cluster.
- Build a prospect list of 50 businesses.
- Create a short audit template:
  - homepage conversion issues
  - trust issues
  - mobile issues
  - Google Business Profile issues
  - missing CTA or booking flow
- Prepare one core offer deck or one-page PDF.

### Week 2

- Send 10 first-touch messages per weekday.
- Send 5 follow-ups per weekday.
- Record 1 personalized audit per weekday for the best leads.
- Book 3 to 5 calls.

### Week 3

- Run sales calls.
- Recommend the `Business` plan unless there is a clear reason not to.
- Close 1 to 2 clients.
- Start delivery fast and ask for a review as soon as value is visible.

### Week 4

- Review which outreach messages got replies.
- Double down on the best-performing niche and angle.
- Ask live clients for referrals and reviews.
- Upsell SEO or AI to the first clients.

## Quick Qualification Rules

Prioritize businesses that match at least 3 of these:

- outdated website
- weak mobile layout
- slow or confusing homepage
- no booking or weak CTA
- few recent reviews
- incomplete Google Business Profile
- strong service demand in their market
- clear ability to pay $149+/mo

Skip leads that:

- already have a polished high-converting site
- are not actively operating
- have no obvious contact path
- serve markets far outside your current geography unless fully remote is intentional

## Outreach Workflow

### First Touch

Subject: quick idea for [Business Name]

```text
Hey [Name], I took a quick look at [Business Name] and found a few things on your site and Google presence that could help you get more calls and bookings.

I run Spiffy Tec. We build and manage websites for service businesses on a simple monthly plan, and I think [specific issue] is probably costing you leads right now.

Want me to send the short audit?
```

### Follow-Up

```text
Hey [Name], following up here.

The biggest issue I noticed was [one issue]. It is fixable pretty quickly, and I can show you exactly how I would improve it.

Want the audit?
```

### DM Version

```text
Hey, I checked out your website and found a few things that are probably costing you calls. If you want, I can send a short audit with the fixes I would make.
```

## Sales Call Structure

Use this flow:

1. Ask how they get leads now.
2. Ask where leads drop off.
3. Ask what one extra booked job is worth.
4. Show the top 3 issues hurting trust or conversion.
5. Recommend the best plan.
6. Close on setup and first payment.

Closing line:

```text
Based on what you told me, I would recommend the Business plan at $149/month because you need a stronger website, a better conversion path, and ongoing updates without hiring someone in-house.
```

## Proposal Structure
